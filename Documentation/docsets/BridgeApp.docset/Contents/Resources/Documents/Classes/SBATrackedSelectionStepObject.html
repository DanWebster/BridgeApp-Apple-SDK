SBATrackedSelectionStepObject

open class SBATrackedSelectionStepObject : RSDUIStepObject, SBATrackedItemsStep

SBATrackedSelectionStepObject is intended for use in selecting items from a long, sectioned list. In general, this would be the first step in setting up tracked data such as symptoms of a disease or triggers associated with a medical condition.
